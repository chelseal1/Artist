Kehlani Parrish




Background

Kehlani is a Oakland native, she was born on April 24th 1995 which makes her a Taurus. Kehlani is known to be a an R&B and Hip/Hop artist. She is a singer-songwriter and a dancer who is currently signed to Atlanic Records. Kehlani rose to fame after appearing on America's Got Talent with her band at the time PopLyfe.

Fun Facts

  • Kehlani cousin is rapper Clyde Carson
  • Kehlani once dated Kyrie Irving
  • Kehlani describes her ethnicity as a mix of Black, White, Native America, Spanish, and Filipino
  • When she was younger Kehlani aspired to be a dancer. She wanted to attend Juilliard school
  • Kehlani finished in fourth place during the 4th season of America's Got Talent
